Frequently Asked Questions

How long does concrete take to cure?

Concrete typically takes 24-48 hours to set, but full curing can take up to 28 days for maximum strength.

What's the difference between concrete and cement?

Cement is an ingredient in concrete. Concrete is a mixture of cement, sand, gravel, and water.

Can I customize the look of my concrete?

Yes! We offer decorative concrete options, including stamped, stained, and polished finishes.

How long does a concrete driveway last?

With proper maintenance, a concrete driveway can last 30+ years.

Do you offer commercial concrete services?

Absolutely! We handle everything from small residential projects to large-scale commercial concrete jobs.

How soon can I use my new concrete surface?

Light foot traffic is usually okay after 24-48 hours, but wait at least 7 days before driving on it.
